About the App

Joe’s TPMS is a free Android application designed to monitor tire pressure using supported Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) TPMS sensors. The app provides real-time pressure data and visual status indicators to help drivers maintain proper tire inflation.

Users can create vehicle profiles, assign individual sensors to tire positions, and configure custom alert thresholds for low or high pressure alerts.

Joe’s TPMS also supports Android Auto, allowing compatible vehicles to display tire pressure information directly on the car’s infotainment screen for safer, hands-free monitoring while driving.
