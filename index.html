Compatible Bluetooth TPMS Sensors

Joe’s TPMS is designed to work with specific BLE tire sensors. The following sensor has been tested and verified for compatibility:

  • BLE External Tire Pressure Monitoring Sensor (AliExpress)

About the App

Joe’s TPMS helps you monitor tire pressure (and supported telemetry) from compatible BLE sensors. Use profiles to assign sensors to positions and receive alerts based on your thresholds.
